Welcome to BINI's Portfolio

BINI is an eight-member Filipino girl group formed by ABS-CBN’s Star Hunt Academy. They officially debuted in June 2021 with their single “Born to Win.” Known for their vibrant and empowering music, BINI has quickly gained popularity in the P-pop scene.

About BINI

The group consists of Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena. BINI’s name symbolizes courage, strength, and resilience, inspired by the Filipino word “binibini,” meaning a young lady. With their catchy songs, synchronized performances, and distinct personalities, they continue to captivate fans both locally and internationally.
